Your Business Deserves a Smarter Server

SDS (Smart Dedicated Server) by AVX is a next-generation hosting solution designed for businesses that demand the power of dedicated servers with the flexibility and intelligence of cloud technology. Engineered for performance, security, and efficiency, SDS delivers a fully managed, high-performance environment tailored to your exact needs.

Benefits

The Benefits of Smart Dedicated Server with AVX

Eliminate Maintenance Issues

Say goodbye to power outages, bandwidth limitations, and server downtime.

Seamless Remote Access

Access your Tally application via RDP from various operating systems, including Linux, Mac, Windows, and iOS.

Secure Data Handling

Our data centers are located in India, allowing you to activate your Indian Tally License securely.

Daily Automated Backups

Automatic cloud backups ensure your Tally data is always safe and easily recoverable in case of accidental loss.

Multi-User Collaboration

Allow multiple users to work on the same Tally data simultaneously, improving team productivity and real-time accuracy.

Role-Based Access Control

Assign specific permissions to users, controlling who can view, edit, or manage different parts of your Tally data.

Real-Time Data Syncs

Changes made by one user are instantly visible to others — no delays or data mismatches.
